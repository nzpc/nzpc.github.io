## Eligibility requirements

This category is open to anyone living in New Zealand at the time of the contest who has had no part in preparing any of the problem set.

## Registration

To register, a team requires a team manager who will undertake to pay the entry fee for the team (typically $120, but the amount is set per site &ndash; confirm with your site coordinator). Note that there is no fee if the contest is on-line. The team manager may be one of the contestants.

Team and contestant registration will be carried out on the contest day before the practice session.

## Teams

A team may contain up to 3 contestants. If space is limited at a site, teams with 3 contestants will be given priority over smaller teams.

Team managers may reserve space for their teams &ndash; usually through TeamUp &ndash; as described on the [Registration page](/register/); check with your site coordinator.
