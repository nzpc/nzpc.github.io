Registration is handled per site

Registering ahead of time mainly helps your site plan catering and seating – it is not required. You are welcome to simply turn up on the day and register your team directly on the }}" target="_blank" rel="noopener">contest server.

The method of registration and the cost both depend on your site, so please contact your site coordinator (listed below) for the details at your venue.

Usually, teams reserve places through }}" target="_blank" rel="noopener">TeamUp using the code {{< siteparam "teamupCode" >}}. Your site coordinator will confirm whether this applies and what the entry fee is.

For questions about a particular site – the venue, local arrangements, reserving places or the entry fee – contact the site coordinator below.
